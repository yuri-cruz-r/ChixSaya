Our Story: All Around the World to the Philippines

Bringing unique chicken flavors from around the world straight to the Filipino community.

Where It All Began

Chix Saya started with a simple goal — to serve chicken dishes inspired by flavors from other countries, brought home to the Philippines by the owners themselves. Our shop is known for its straightforward, no-frills setup, focusing on what truly matters: great food and great flavor.

Unlike other unli-wings places, Chix Saya doesn’t rely on air-conditioned interiors or fancy features. Instead, we let our flavors speak for themselves. Our best-selling Soy Garlic, crafted to be perfectly balanced and not overly salty, has become a favorite for both kids and adults.

From families to students, we continue to serve the community with freshly cooked meals and a dining experience that feels warm, simple, and genuinely satisfying — “Mas Masarap, Mas Masaya.”

Though we mostly promote through our Facebook page and simple posts, the heart of Chix Saya lives in the community itself. And as we continue to grow, we hold on to our message and tagline: “Mas Masarap, Mas Masaya.” Because for us, good food should always bring good moments — shared, enjoyed, and remembered.

Our Mission & Values

Authentic Global Flavors

Our sauces and flavors are inspired by recipes from other countries — brought home to the Philippines by the owners to create a unique taste you won’t find anywhere else.

Simple, Honest Dining

No fancy setup, no unnecessary extras. We focus on serving good chicken, good flavor, and a straightforward dining experience.

Freshly Cooked Always

Every order is cooked on the spot to ensure crispness, balanced flavor, and the signature Chix Saya satisfaction.

For Families & Students

Our shop mainly serves families and students — the heart of our community and the people we love cooking for.

Community & Local Growth

We stay connected with our neighborhood through simple promotions and Facebook updates, continuously growing with the community we serve.

Our Tagline

“Mas Masarap, Mas Masaya” — the guiding message behind every dish we make.
